Frequently Asked Questions

What services does Nexos Medical provide?
Nexos Medical offers general consultations, specialist care, diagnostic testing, minor procedures, and telemedicine.
Do I need an appointment, or can I walk in?
While walk-ins are welcome, we recommend booking an appointment to reduce wait time and ensure availability.
Is telemedicine available?
Yes, Nexos Medical offers secure virtual consultations with our doctors. Book online or call us to schedule.
Do you accept health insurance?
We accept most major health insurance providers. Please contact us to confirm coverage before your visit.
How do I get my lab test results?
Results are shared via our secure patient portal or can be collected in person. You’ll be notified when they're ready.

Still Have Questions?

Our care team is here to help—reach out 24/7 via phone, email, or visit our help center.
